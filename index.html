Waves in the electromagnetic spectrum can travel many millions of light years.

Waves on the ocean can circle the earth.

Their power is never lost but is absorbed by other energy systems.

Similarly, the waves of our global communications can carry powerful messages.

How we as humans absorb these messages is key to both our growth as a species and our lasting peace.

We cannot be in phase with all of the 7 billion plus ideas on how we should live.

Neither should we blindly align ourselves with one powerful idea. History shows this to be disastrous.

Democracy is not perfect but the consensus rules. Dangerous minority views are restricted and positive minority views are tolerated, encouraged and celebrated.

We will not stabilize our society by rejecting and discrediting ideas just because they are not our own.

We can confront opinions with our own arguments but must also accept that we are often wrong and have to compromise.

We can learn to try to understand differing ideas, accept the reasons behind them and that they are as valid as our own.

We can discuss and debate them in a tolerant and open minded way.

Global Game Jam has shown how diverse, talented and interesting people can come together, accept each other and work in union.

We can pool our ideas, skills and efforts and make something wonderful that did not exist before.

It's not easy; ideas and views change constantly, but with open minds and open hearts...

...we can all be on the same wave.
